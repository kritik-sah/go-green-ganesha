Q1. Does an eco-friendly Ganesha idol dissolve in water?

  • Yes. if you buy a mud ganesha or tree ganesha, it will dissolve completely in water.

Q2. Can we grow plants in the same soil?

  • Yes. The soil used for making the idols is 100% natural and contains no harmful chemicals or colors. Hence, it is ideal for growing small plants in your balcony, kitchen or backyard.

Q3. Do I anyway contribute to the environment by buying eco-friendly ganesha idols?

  • Yes. Buying eco-friendly ganesha idols has the below benefits to the environment:

    1. Does not pollute natural water sources
    2. Made from red soil which can be used to grow plants
    3. Visarjan & Tree plantation at the same time
    4. Dissolves completely and leaves no residue
    5. Causes no harm to marine life

Q4. Is go green ganesha a retailer for ganesh idols?

  • No. We only connect potential customers to the best sellers in the market.

Q5. Does Go Green Ganesha initiative includes any for profit arrangement?

  • No. We are a squad to support the cause for saving our environment from any harm during the festival. Hence, you can find the sellers for eco-friendly ganesha idols here and compare the rates and designs from multiple vendors to choose the best and support the cause at the same time.

Q6. How can we identify eco-friendly Ganpati?

  • It is very light to carry and has bright colours on it. One made with clay is heavy and will have a slight dull appearance.

Q7. Why is it important to celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi in an Eco-friendly way?

  • Switching to Eco-friendly means, we are being responsible citizens doing our little bit in saving and nurturing the environment. By using Eco-friendly idols, we spare and secure our nature and help it bloom and flourish.

Q8. What can we do with the sludge collected after immersion?

  • The sludge that collects can be poured into a garden nearby. In this way the intention of the worship ritual reaches back to the earth.
